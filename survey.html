Silent Screamer Beta Reader Survey

Thank you for being a beta reader for Silent Screamer. I'm asking for honest, specific feedback — not a line edit and not a professional editorial report. What matters most is your reader experience: where it gripped you, where it slowed, what stayed with you, and whether the ending felt earned.

The survey takes around 15–20 minutes. No question is compulsory, but the more specific you can be, the more useful it will be.
