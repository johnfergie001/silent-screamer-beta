Thank you for agreeing to be a beta reader for my book Silent Screamer — I really appreciate it. First and foremost I really hope you enjoy it!

As part of the feedback survey, I'm asking for your honest, unvarnished feedback. My goal is simple: the better this book becomes, the more people will read it. The more people read it, the greater the awareness of the subject matter. So if something didn't work for you, please say so. You're not hurting my feelings — you're helping me make it count.

That said, unless you're a world-class editor — or happen to know one — I'm not looking for it to be torn apart either 🙂 Honest, thoughtful, and specific is exactly what I need...and thanks again for your time 🙂

The survey has 8 short sections and takes around 20 minutes. No question is compulsory — skip anything that doesn't apply to you.
